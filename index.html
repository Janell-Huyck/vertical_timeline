Click-Handlers

Aug 2019

This is my first prject that dealt with interacting with the user. The user is clicking on different buttons to 1) change the color of a box, 2) increment a counter, and 3) determine if the value of that counter is even or odd. It was also my first exposure to CSS, and we were given examples to follow with the color changes and making the buttons.
